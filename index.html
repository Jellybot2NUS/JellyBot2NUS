Capabilities

Built for the
deep blue

Jellybot integrates cutting-edge engineering with biomimetic principles, creating a platform that is as elegant as the creature it emulates.

Mission-Ready Housing

Utilizes an industrial-grade, depth-rated enclosure and precision O-ring sealing to ensure total hermetic integrity in high-pressure marine environments.

High-Res Imaging

Provides crystal-clear underwater imaging for coral reef and ecosystem observation.

Smart Navigation

Integrated sensor array for obstacle detection and stable, precise control underwater.

Energy Efficient

Biomimetic propulsion mimics jellyfish motion for sustainable, low-impact ocean exploration.

Modular Subsystems

Every module — mechanical, electrical, propulsion, electronics — can be independently upgraded or replaced.

Eco-Friendly

Designed to minimise acoustic disturbance and energy consumption, reducing environmental footprint.
