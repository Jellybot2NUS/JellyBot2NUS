Features of Jellybot

Durable Design

Built with waterproof PVC housing and a fully sealed structure for underwater endurance.

Camera Dome

Provides clear underwater imaging for coral and ecosystem observation.

Smart Navigation

Integrated sensors for obstacle detection and stable control underwater.

Energy Efficient

Biomimetic propulsion mimics jellyfish motion for sustainable ocean exploration.

Modular Subsystems

Each subsystem — mechanical, electrical, propulsion, and electronics — can be independently upgraded or replaced for flexible maintenance.

Eco-Friendly Operation

Designed with sustainability in mind, Jellybot minimizes noise and energy consumption to reduce its environmental impact.
